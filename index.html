Data Sources & Collection

PC + Console Data

Source: NewZoo.

Period: Fixed (January 2023 – December 2025).

Metrics: Total MAU (sum across each month during the period) drives the Mekko and market-share visuals. Peak MAU (highest single-month MAU observed in 2023–2025) drives the per-title Top 10 rankings.

Titles: ~4,700 games with measurable MAU.

Switch Data

Source: NewZoo.

Period: Fixed (January 2023 – December 2025).

Merge logic: For titles on both PC/Console and Switch, we take the max MAU per (Game, Genre, Archetype) tuple to avoid double-counting.

Roblox Data

Source: Scraped from the Roblox website.

Period: Snapshot as of early 2026.

Metric: Concurrent Users (CCU) — a point-in-time measure, not comparable 1:1 with MAU.

Important caveat: PC + Console uses cumulative peak MAU (monthly engagement), while Roblox uses CCU (real-time concurrency). The dashboard compares share distributions and proportional patterns, not absolute values.

Genre Taxonomy

Genres are assigned per title from source data classification. The dashboard uses 17–18 genre categories depending on the platform. Genres with zero total are automatically excluded.

Visualization Methodology

Marimekko (Mekko) Chart

Column width = genre's share of total engagement. Vertical stack = archetype breakdown within that genre. Bottom 7 genres grouped as "Other" at default zoom; zooming past 1.5x expands them. Supports up to 300x zoom with pan.

Pie / Donut Charts

Summary donuts with polyline leader lines for label readability. Slices below 2.5% have labels hidden. Double-click any slice for Top 20 drill-down.

Concentration Chart (Log₂ Ratio)

log₂(PC+Console share / Roblox share). +1 means PC+Console has 2x proportional weight; −1 means Roblox has 2x. Balanced log scale prevents visual domination.

Radar / Spider Chart

Overlays archetype share distribution for both platforms. Radial axis = share of total engagement.

Divergence Chart

Raw percentage-point difference (Roblox share − PC+Console share). Green = Roblox proportionally larger; Blue = PC+Console dominant.
